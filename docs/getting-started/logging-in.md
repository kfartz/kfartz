# Logging In

## Accessing Kfartz

Navigate to the Kfartz URL provided by your administrator. If you're not already logged in, you'll be redirected to the login page.

![](../assets/login.avif)

## Login Process

1. Enter your email address
2. Enter your password
3. Click **Log In**

If you've forgotten your password, contact your administrator to have it reset.

## Logging Out

To log out:

- Use the keyboard shortcut `Ctrl+L` (or `Cmd+L` on Mac)
- Or click your user menu and select Log Out
